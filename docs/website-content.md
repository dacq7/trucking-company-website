# INSURANCE SERVICES

Section Title:
Insurance Solutions for Trucking Businesses

Section Description:
We provide specialized insurance coverage designed to protect trucking companies, drivers, and transported cargo.

Services:

Auto Liability (AL)

Auto Liability insurance is required for commercial trucks operating on public highways. This coverage protects your business in case of bodily injury or property damage caused to others in an accident.

Motor Truck Cargo (MTC)

Motor Truck Cargo insurance protects the goods being transported by your truck. It covers losses caused by theft, accidents, or damage during transit.

Physical Damage (APD)

Physical Damage insurance protects your truck against collisions, fire, vandalism, and natural disasters. It helps cover repair or replacement costs.

General Liability (GL)

General Liability insurance protects your business from claims related to third-party injuries or property damage that occur during your operations.

Workers Compensation

Workers Compensation insurance provides protection for employees in case of workplace injuries, covering medical expenses and lost wages.

# REGULATORY SERVICES

Section Title:
DOT Compliance & Regulatory Services

Section Description:
Operating a trucking company requires compliance with several federal regulations. Our team helps ensure that your business remains fully compliant with all required filings and registrations.

Regulatory Services:

DOT Number Registration

We assist trucking companies in obtaining their official DOT Number required by the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration.

MC Number (Motor Carrier Authority)

Motor Carrier Authority allows companies to transport goods across state lines legally.

Unified Carrier Registration (UCR)

UCR is an annual registration required for interstate motor carriers operating in the United States.

BOC-3 Filing

BOC-3 filing designates legal agents in every state to receive official legal documents on behalf of your trucking company.

IRP Registration

International Registration Plan (IRP) allows commercial vehicles to operate across multiple states with a single registration.

IFTA Registration

International Fuel Tax Agreement (IFTA) simplifies fuel tax reporting for trucking companies operating in multiple jurisdictions.

DOT Number Updates

We help update company information associated with your DOT Number when business details change.

Biennial Update (MCS-150)

The FMCSA requires companies to update their registration information every two years through the MCS-150 form.

Operating Authority Reinstatement

If your operating authority has been suspended, we assist in restoring it quickly so you can continue operating.

Drug & Alcohol Consortium Enrollment

Commercial drivers must participate in a federally approved drug and alcohol testing program. We help companies enroll and remain compliant.
